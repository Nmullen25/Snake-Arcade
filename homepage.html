Play The Game

To Get Started:

Select a Difficulty:

  • Easy: 200 milliseconds
  • Intermediate: 100 milliseconds
  • Hard: 75 milliseconds

Press play to start the eating spree:

A timer will countdown to the start and the snake will slither


When your snake bites itself or hits a wall:

The game will stop because the snake can't eat those...

Press the Reset button to play again

If you beat the high score, Congrats, try a harder speed.

If not, try harder...
